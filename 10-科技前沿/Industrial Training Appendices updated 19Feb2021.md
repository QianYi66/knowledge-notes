# APPENDICES – LOG BOOK & REPORT
## INDUSTRIAL TRAINING LOG BOOK
### TRAINING INFORMATION
Instructions to Student
- Industrial Training Log Book (hereafter “the log book”) has been designed to record reflections concerning the student’s learning opportunities, experiences and achievements gained at the workplace.

- A Student must handwrite/type clearly all the assigned tasks, problems faced, details of discussion and jobs accomplished during training in the log book.

- The log book’s format cannot be changed.

- The log book must be handed to the Industrial Supervisor for verification on a weekly basis.

- All the signed log books need to be attached together with the Industrial Training Report in hardcopy under the Appendices Chapter and submitted to the Academic Supervisor.
